    Know Me Better



    [Legal Name:Ayush Sharma]


    Hi, I'm Ayush Hardeniya (also known as Ayush Sharma) - a student, web creator, and lifelong learner passionate about technology, design, and the art of digital self-expression.

    I love building meaningful things that solve real problems and reflect intentional design. Here's a quick glimpse into my journey sp far - driven by curiosity, vision, and heart. 🌱




    2006 – 2011

    Birth & Roots

    The Beginning

    Born in July 2006, into a family that’s the very foundation of who I am. These were the days of pure innocence and warmth.

    2011 – 2015

    Village Life

    River Mornings & Family Joy

    The time when life was simple, days were long, and family was the celebration. Early 4 AM trips to the river bridge with Grandpa became the roots of discipline and values.

    2015 – 2019

    The CBS Phase

    Real Learning Begins

    This wasn’t just school — it was the real learning ground. Projects, events, leadership, and even a memorable visit to IISF Delhi. From Roman numeral crafts to acting and dance, it was all alive here.

    2019 – 2023

    From 10th to 12th

    Growth, Loss & Grit

    Scoring above 96% in 10th, balancing 70 km school commutes with my brother, and facing the pain of losing Grandpa in Dec 2021. These years defined resilience, both emotional and academic.

    2023 – Present

    The Launchpad

    Execution Era

    Entering college — not just a new place, but a new life. Wearing the uniform again, learning to lead, finding my purpose, and making real moves toward becoming the best version of Ayush Hardeniya.
